Imperfect Gamers: Foxhole Guide

Welcome to Foxhole

NOTE: This is a guide compiled by the Imperfect Gamers Community. Although most of the guide is authored by members of the Imperfect Gamers, there will be many instances of referencing external sources and images. All the sources can be found at the end of the guide.

So you decided to join the never-ending wars of Foxhole? Excellent! This guide will assume you are familiar with the premise of Foxhole, and will jump straight into how to play this amazing game.

Please note that although most of the guide will apply to both factions, some aspects will only be relevant to players on the Warden side. This is due to the Imperfect (Gamers) Regiment consists of Warden loyalists. It is not in the current plans to write this guide without factional bias, but if there is high demand, it is possible that the Imperfect Community rewrites it.

About Imperfect Gamers

We are a community of people who enjoy playing video games. We try to unite like-minded people through video games, and we try to amplify the fun of each member through community engagement.

About the Imperfect Regiment

The Imperfect Regiment is a regiment created by us (Imperfect Gamers) in Foxhole. We do not have any specific area of expertise in the game, instead we try to do what's fun and what we feel like. This does not mean we do not organize or plan to organize big regiment facilities, special operations, bunker-bases etc.
